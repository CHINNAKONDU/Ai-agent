
## Usage

Enter a village, mandal, or district name:
- Example: "Lakkireddipalle, Kadapa"
- Example: "Vempalle, Andhra Pradesh"
- Example: "Pulivendla"

## Technology Stack
- **Python** - Flask Framework
- **HTML** - Structure
- **CSS** - Styling (Dark Mode)
- **JavaScript** - Frontend Logic
- **SQLite** - Database
- **Open-Meteo API** - Weather Data

## Safety Note
Weather conditions may change rapidly. Always verify with local authorities during severe weather conditions.
